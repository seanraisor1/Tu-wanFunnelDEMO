Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a Christian to work with you? +

While my coaching is faith-based, I welcome clients from all backgrounds. The spiritual component focuses on finding purpose and meaning in your transformation journey.

What if I'm a complete beginner? +

Perfect! I specialize in meeting you exactly where you are. Every program is customized to your current fitness level and experience.

How does the coaching work remotely? +

Through weekly video calls, custom app-based programs, constant text support, and progress tracking. You'll feel like I'm right there with you.

What's included in the monthly fee? +

Everything you need: custom workout plans, nutrition guidance, weekly check-ins, 24/7 text support, mindset coaching, and spiritual growth sessions.

Is there a commitment period? +

I recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for real transformation, but we can discuss what works best for your situation during our initial call.
